⭐Rules
 
 🎮 Game Objective:
Be the first player to reach 100 points on the main score!

🕹 How to Play:

1️⃣ Roll the Dice

Click “Roll Dice”

The number on the dice is added to your current score

You can keep rolling to increase your current score

2️⃣ But be careful…

If you roll a 1 🚫
→ You lose your current score
→ Your turn automatically switches to the other player

3️⃣ Hold Your Score

Click “Hold”

Your current score gets added to your total score

Your turn switches to the other player

A safe move when you fear rolling a 1!

4️⃣ Winning the Game 🏆

The first player to reach 100 points or more wins the game

The winner gets highlighted with a special style 🎉

5️⃣ Start New Game

Click “New Game” anytime to reset everything and play again
